Built for the everyday shop owner

StockFlow was born out of a simple observation — most small store owners in local communities still rely on paper notebooks, manual counting, and gut instinct to run their businesses. We built StockFlow to change that, without requiring technical expertise or expensive hardware.

    📋 Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I add a new product to inventory?

    Go to the Inventory page and fill out the "Add New Product" form at the top. Enter the product name, category, price, initial stock, and an emoji icon, then click Save to Inventory.

    Can I export my sales data?

    Yes! On the Dashboard, click the Export CSV button in the top-right corner. This downloads your current inventory and sales summary as a CSV file.

    How do I change a user's role?

    Currently, roles are assigned when a user is created. To change a role, delete the user from the Users page and re-create them with the desired role (Admin, Manager, or Cashier).

    Where is my data stored?

    All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It persists across sessions but is limited to the current browser and device. Clearing browser data will erase your StockFlow data.

    How do I print a receipt?

    After completing a sale in the Point of Sale, a receipt modal appears. Click Print Receipt to use your browser's print function. Business plan users can customize the store name and tagline in Settings → Receipt Branding.

    What does the "Low Stock" badge mean?

    Products with 10 or fewer units are flagged as "Low Stock" with an orange badge. Products with 0 units are marked as "Out of Stock" in red.
